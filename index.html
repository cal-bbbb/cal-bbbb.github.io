about

Welcome to my site. I've always been passionate about the way things were designed. I first started with website design. I loved being able to manipulate the foundations of what I was shown on my screen. I also started playing around with making music, which I fell in love with immediately. I was hungry to understand what made a good song, a good website, a good story. My curiousity led me to study business and design at Northeastern University with a concentration in marketing.

Currently, I'm working on Soundsteer, an AI-enhanced digital management platform targeted towards independent artists that allows them to create social campaigns, track their progress, submit to playlists, and more.

Before that? I spent my time building projects and products of all types. I know I left some links to them somewhere around here...
